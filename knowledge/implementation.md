# Implementation Priorities and Success Metrics

## 8.1 Quick Wins (First 30 Days)

- Publish a **"How NMTCs Work"** educational carousel targeting developers. This is the highest-ROI first post: it attracts the most profitable segment and fills the biggest content gap in the market.
- Write a **"What NMTC Permanency Means"** analysis post targeting investors. Timely, high-value, and demonstrates market intelligence.
- Share **one impact story** with a before/after photo and financial specifics. This is the brand's signature content type and should be introduced early.
- Audit team members' LinkedIn profiles and align them with the Galvanize brand voice. **Employee-generated content gets 2x the engagement** of company page posts.

## 8.2 Foundation Building (Days 31–90)

- Launch a recurring **"NMTC/HTC Explained"** carousel series (biweekly). Build a library of evergreen educational content that compounds over time.
- Create the **first gated resource** (NMTC Eligibility Checklist or HTC Project Qualification Guide) and promote it through a DM-capture post.
- Publish the **first allocation round analysis** post following the CY 2026 round opening. Position Galvanize as the go-to source for allocation intelligence.
- Begin a **"Community Spotlight"** series featuring one project per month with the community's voice centered.

## 8.3 Scale & Convert (Days 91–180)

- Launch a **quarterly market brief** (PDF or email) offered to investors and CDEs. This becomes the primary conversion tool for the two highest-value segments.
- Host a **LinkedIn Live or webinar**: "Tax Credits 101 for Community Leaders" targeting Segment 4. Capture registrants as leads and referral sources.
- Test a **contrarian thought leadership post** targeting investors: something that takes a non-obvious position on credit pricing, program effectiveness, or market direction.
- **Measure and optimize:** track follower growth by segment, engagement rate by content pillar, DMs generated, and inbound advisory inquiries sourced from social.

## 8.4 Success Metrics

| Metric | 6-Month Target | 12-Month Target |
|--------|----------------|-----------------|
| LinkedIn followers (company page) | 1,500–2,500 | 5,000+ |
| Average engagement rate per post | 3–5% | 5–7% |
| Monthly DMs/inquiries from content | 8–15 | 25–40 |
| Gated resource downloads | 100–200 | 500+ |
| Advisory engagements sourced from social | 2–4 | 8–12 |
| Employee advocacy participation rate | 50% of team posting monthly | 80% posting biweekly |

Use these metrics when evaluating content performance and when the Engagement Analyzer agent recommends optimizations.
